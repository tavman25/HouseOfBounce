Info

Professional rentals with a playful spirit.

What we offer

Bright bounce houses and party inflatables for birthdays, schools, churches, fundraisers, and community events across northern Maine.

Service area

We provide free setup, removal, and cleaning within 30 miles of Houlton, Maine. We also travel up to 100 miles.

Simple pricing policy

For events more than 30 miles from Houlton, we charge a flat $50 delivery and removal fee. No extra cleaning, water, or hidden service fees.

Power support included

We provide the electrical cords needed for setup, and we can supply a generator when required at no additional charge.
